To help fight #COVID19BE we've already created thousands of back-up face masks for our care providers. Handmade, with love.

Make and wear a reusable face mask yourself, when and where the government recommends it. Keep a distance of at least 1.5 m, don't touch your face and wash your hands with soap and water.

Make your own face mask

  • wash your hands for 60 seconds with soap and water

  • clean your sewing machine thoroughly

  • wash your hands again before sewing your face mask

  • wear a face mask while making your own face mask

Wear your face mask

Are you or is someone you live with sick? Are you in the supermarket or in a poorly-ventilated place? Are you a high-risk patient? There are benefits to wearing a face mask, but only if you wear it properly.

For people who rely on visual communication

Do you rely on lipreading? Do you need to see people's mouth, or their mimicry? Do you need to be able to read emotions from faces? Then this pattern for a face mask with a clear window is exactly what you were looking for.
